Mathematics / Category Theory

Definition is the best tool used for our peace, making rules that everyone is to approve of.
We have to learn Mathematics because we are living together in this world.
When I was a student of Japanese Top Lanked University, I feel that the education of Matrices (Linear Algebra) are made with pencil and papers, which will make the students spoil their time, which means that we need more abstract theory of them. With Category Theory, we can handle any kind of matrices with understanding of algebra using category. The furthermore (Formal) Category Theory can be a language because it has varieties which current or past "Set Theory" (sets with functions) does not have.
