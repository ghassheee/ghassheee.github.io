The concept


Whoever defined as a person has two legs and two hands and one head?
Some person could happen not to have two legs, but three legs.
Joan Milo draws a picture of a person with strange legs.
It might be a miracle that we usually have two legs.
Therefore, we must be careful when we construct a house.
It should be much more miracle one.
This is the starting point of contemporary Architecture.
Poles or Walls can happen to move as its dweller wants.
This is our starting point of the view.
