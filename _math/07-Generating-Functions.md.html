# Fibonacci Numbers


It might be famous that nth fibonacci number $fib_n$ can be solved with complex analysis.
we get nth fibonnaci number just by dumping $$f(z) = \frac{-z}{z^2 + z - 1}$$ to the residue calculus. ( We might get $fib_n = \frac{1}{\alpha^n(\alpha-\beta)} + \frac{1}{\beta^n(\beta-\alpha)}$ where $\alpha, \beta$ are zeros of $z^2 + z - 1$ . )
