## Definition
Axiom of Choice (AC) : Every family of nonempty sets has a choice function.

## Formally
$ X $ : an arbitrary set
$ \mathscr{F} $ : a family of $ S \ ;\ S \subset X $

### Axiom of Choice :
$$ \forall \mathscr{F}.\ \left[ \begin{array}{} \emptyset \notin \mathscr{F}\\ \ \\ \hline \\ \forall S \in \mathscr{F}. \exists f : \mathscr{F} \rightarrow \cup_{S \in \mathscr{F}} S.\quad f(S) \in S \end{array} \right] $$

### Another definition :
$$ \forall \mathscr{F}. \ \left[ \begin{array}{} \emptyset \notin \mathscr{F}\\ \ \\ \hline\\ \prod_{S \in \mathscr{F}} S \neq \emptyset \end{array} \right] $$
